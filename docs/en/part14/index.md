# Part 14: Practical Projects

## Positioning

Part 14 turns the methods from the first twelve parts and the open-source model data recipes from Part 13 into fifteen runnable projects. Together they form an end-to-end practice loop around pre-training, SFT, multimodal data, RAG, agents, DataOps, privacy protection, reasoning flywheels, and video-generation data pipelines.

## Suggested Reading Order

- Read P01 through P15 in order, or select projects according to the theme of the preceding chapters.
- For fast reproduction, start with P01, P02, and P05.
- For platform construction, focus on the relationship among P07, P08, P09, and P10.
- For reproducing open-source large-model data recipes, focus on P11 through P14 and reread Part 13 Chapters 44 through 48.
