# Part 13: Open-Source Large-Model Data Engineering Recipes and Paradigms

## Positioning

Part 13 focuses on data recipes, training paradigms, and engineering organization for open-source large models. It covers pre-training, post-training, reasoning reinforcement learning, VLMs, and T2I/T2V. The emphasis is on extracting transferable frameworks from public technical reports, open-source model practice, and data-pipeline design.

## Suggested Reading Order

- Read Chapters 44 and 45 first to establish the current coordinates of open-source pre-training and post-training recipes.
- Then read Chapters 46 through 48 to understand how reasoning models, VLMs, and multimodal generation diverge in data engineering.
- For hands-on reproduction, continue to Part 14 Projects 11 through 14, connecting recipes, flywheels, multimodal instruction, and video-generation datasets into implementation paths.
